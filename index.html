A Bit About Us

Dhiman Shuttering House had started as a small store with a few hundred products in a small shelter in sector 7 Panchkula. Started by Shri Bidhi Chand Dhiman in 1975, we have been in business since a few decades now with a conglomerate of products to offer for rental.
